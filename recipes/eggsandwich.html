EGG SANDWICH

Description:

At the heart of a great egg sandwich is a creamy egg filling and essential to this are soft boiled, not hard boiled eggs! The creamy yolks practically makes its own mayonnaise sauce and the soft-set egg whites almost melt in your mouth.

The result is a handheld breakfast option that combines the richness and protein of eggs with the comforting simplicity of bread. Egg sandwiches are quick and easy to make, making them a convenient choice for busy mornings or whenever you crave a tasty and filling meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Slices Bread
  • 3 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • 10 g Butter

Steps:

  1. In a large frying melt the butter on low to medium heat.
  2. Crack the eggs into a separate bowl then whisk and add to the pan ensuring that the mixture spreads all over the pan.
  3. Immediately add the slices of bread into the pan then once the egg had coated the bottom of the bread turn over the bread slice immediately â€“ make sure that you leave a little gap in between the two slices so that they can be flipped on top of one another.
  4. Cook the egg for 2-3 minutes until it has set then once cooked, use a spatula, and slide underneath the bread slides and flip.
  5. Add the cheese, salt, and black pepper along with any toppings of your choice on one of the bread slices then flip over any excess egg on the side.
  6. Flip the toast with no toppings on top of the other create a sandwich then cook for 30 seconds to allow the cheese to bind the two pieces together.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!.
